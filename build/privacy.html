STARTER Hackathon Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

We collect only the information required to administer the STARTER Hackathon, match you with teammates, and keep you informed about event logistics. By joining the event you agree to the practices outlined below.

Information We Collect

  • Registration details, such as your name, email, and organization.
  • Team preferences and project track selections submitted through the form.
  • Optional feedback shared during surveys or mentoring sessions.

How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm your participation and communicate event updates.
  • To coordinate mentors, workshops, judging, and prize eligibility.
  • To improve future hackathons through aggregated, anonymized insights.

Your Choices

You may request data removal or export at any time by emailing privacy@starter.com. We honor opt-out requests for future communications within five business days.

Data Retention & Security

Registration records are retained for one year after the event and deleted afterward. Access is limited to the organizing team and stored using encrypted services.

For questions about this policy contact privacy@starter.com.
